RWBY (pronounced Ruby) is an American anime web series created by Monty Oum and produced by Rooster Teeth Productions. The first episode premiered on Rooster Teeth’s website on July 18, 2013, and has expanded into three seasons consisting of 40 episodes. Following its initial premiere, the series became a viral hit, spawning several fan creations, cosplays, and community buzz. Sadly, original creator Monty Oum passed away in February 2015, but the community’s love of the show’s creative team has continued to propel the series forward, with a new season launching this fall. RWBY is set in the fictional world of Remnant, where aspiring young people train to become Huntsmen and Huntresses to protect the world from monsters. The show follows the exploits of four major characters: Ruby Rose, Weiss Schnee, Blake Belladonna, and Yang Xiao Long. What is it about RWBY that makes the show so special? The animation is a unique take on 3D animation, with shading and coloring reminiscent of anime shows — a style Rooster Teeth has honed over three seasons, with the animation noticeably improving as time passes. As the plot grows more complex and dark, the tone of the art style has changed, with colors becoming more muted.employs this style to great use with the world of Remnant, which blends fantasy with high technology. In particular, the world of Remnant stands out, with its blend of fantasy high technology, as do the four main characters, each of which is designed around a particular color: red, white, black, and yellow. THE WEAPON COMBINATIONS RWBY features high-octane action scenes that utilize some incredible weaponry. Each character is outfitted with her own special weapon that is often a hybrid of two distinctly different weapon types. For example, Ruby Rose’s signature weapon is called Crescent Rose, a hybrid of a scythe and a sniper rifle (check it out transforming from one to the other above). Some other signature weapons include the Ember Celica, a pair of powerful gauntlets that shoot projectile shells, and Magnhild, a large hammer that can transform into a grenade launcher. Each weapon has its own distinct advantages and disadvantages, which makes every combat scene exciting and compelling. THE CREATOR-COMMUNITY CONNECTION Fandom-Explained-RWBY-Community The strength of the community and fandom of RWBY can be attributed to the amazing connection Rooster Teeth develops with their fans. Rooster Teeth holds a convention called RTX every year in Austin, allowing fans to interact with each other and the creators. They’ve also held a contest for fans to design battle gear for the character Velvet Scarlatina. In addition, the show’s creators take pride in answering community questions, and they frequently hold livestream events as well. And the RWBY fanbase of is vibrant and creative itself, with many fans creating original art and fan projects. An independent developer named Jordan Scott created a RWBY video game entitled RWBY: Grimm Eclipse that Rooster Teeth officially adopted. This creator-fan connection has a big part to do with RWBY’s continued success. Its cast is often divided up into various teams with different motives and goals, the personalities of characters may grow and shift throughout the course of each season.
